J&S Studios

A collective collaboration of beloved musicians in Ceylon, comes a humble, handsomely equipped recording & rehearsal studio for young aspiring artists. J & S is focused, to eliminate the technical bottlenecks, resisting local music production. We have high-quality equipment, to help create a variety of music, limited by imagination. J & S Studios' - For the Passionate, Young & Talented.
